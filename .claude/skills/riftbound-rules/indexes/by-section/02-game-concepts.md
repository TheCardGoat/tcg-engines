## Overview
This section covers deck construction requirements, game setup, ownership, and privacy rules.

## 101-103: Deck Construction

### What You Need to Play (Rule 103)

#### 1. Champion Legend (103.1)
- Placed in the Legend Zone at game start
- Dictates the **Domain Identity** of your Main Deck
- Cannot be removed, moved, or displaced during play

#### 2. Main Deck (103.2)
- **Minimum:** 40 cards
- **Includes:** 1 Chosen Champion Unit + Units + Gear + Spells
- **Copy Limit:** Maximum 3 copies of any named card
- **Domain Identity:** All cards must match your Champion Legend's domain(s)
- **Signature Cards:** Maximum 3 total, must match Champion Legend's tag
- **Privacy:** Private information during gameplay

#### 3. Chosen Champion (103.2.a)
- Must be a Champion Unit with a tag matching your Champion Legend
- Placed in Champion Zone at game start
- Counts toward the 3-copy limit
- Can be played from Champion Zone following normal rules

#### 4. Rune Deck (103.3)
- **Exactly:** 12 Rune cards
- Must match Domain Identity
- Kept separate from Main Deck
- Shuffled independently

#### 5. Battlefields (103.4)
- Number determined by Mode of Play
- Subject to Domain Identity

### Domain Identity Rules (103.1.b)
- Single-domain cards: Permitted if domain matches Legend
- Multi-domain cards: ALL domains must be in your Domain Identity
- Can include Domain, Type, Tag, or other attributes

## 104-118: Setup Process

### Setup Steps (Rules 110-118)

1. **Place Champion Legend** (111) - In Legend Zone
2. **Place Chosen Champion** (112) - In Champion Zone
3. **Set Aside Battlefields** (113) - Mode of Play determines usage
4. **Shuffle Decks** (114)
   - Main Deck → Main Deck Zone
   - Rune Deck → Rune Deck Zone
5. **Determine Turn Order** (115) - Fair random method
6. **Draw 4 Cards** (116) - Each player
7. **Mulligan** (117) - In turn order:
   - Choose up to 2 cards to set aside
   - Draw that many cards
   - Recycle the set-aside cards
8. **Begin Play** (118) - First Player takes their turn

### Turn Order (115)
- Established as repeating sequence
- Mode of Play specifies First Player determination
- If not specified, proceeds clockwise from First Player

## 119-123: Game Objects

### Definition (Rule 120)
A Game Object is any game piece that can:
- Produce one or more Game Effects, OR
- Grant prerequisites for players to take Game Actions

### Types of Game Objects (Rule 123)
- Main Deck cards in any zone
- Runes in any zone
- Legends
- Battlefields
- Tokens
- Abilities of any type on the chain
- Buffers and other status markers

### Properties (Rules 121-122)
- Can be literal or logical objects
- No inherent properties beyond producing effects or enabling actions

## 126: Ownership

### Gameplay Ownership (126.1)
- **Owner** = Player who brought the card into the game
- Applies to: Champion Legend, Battlefields, Main Deck cards, Rune Deck cards
- Legal ownership doesn't matter during gameplay

### Example:
If Alice loans Bo a deck, Bo is the owner during that game, even though Alice legally owns the cards.

## 127: Privacy

### Privacy Levels

| Level | Who Can See | Examples |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Secret** (127.3) | No one | Order of cards in decks |
| **Private** (127.4) | Controller/Owner only | Hand, facedown cards |
| **Public** (127.5) | All players | Board state, trash, banishment |

### Key Rules:
- Cards in hand: Private (owner can see)
- Number of cards in hand: Public
- Facedown cards: Private (controller can see)
- Trash contents: Public
- Banishment contents: Public
- Deck order: Secret

## Common Questions

**Q: How many copies of a card can I have in my deck?**
A: Maximum 3 copies of any named card (103.2.b)

**Q: Can my Chosen Champion be a different character than my Legend?**
A: No, the Champion Unit must have a tag matching your Champion Legend (103.2.a.2)

**Q: What happens during mulligan?**
A: Set aside up to 2 cards, draw that many, then recycle the set-aside cards (117)

**Q: Can I look at my opponent's hand?**
A: No, hands are Private information (127.4)

**Q: Can I look through my deck during the game?**
A: No, deck order is Secret unless a card effect allows it (127.3)
