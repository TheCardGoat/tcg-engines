## 1v1 Duel

**Rule refs:** 644

### Setup
- Each player provides **3 Battlefields**
- **Randomly select 1** from each player
- Other 2 are removed (not used)

### First Turn
- Second player channels **extra rune** during first Channel Phase

### Format
- **Best of 1**
- First to 8 points wins

---

## 1v1 Match

**Rule refs:** 645

### Setup
- Each player provides **3 Battlefields**
- **Choose 1** from each player (not random)
- Other 2 set aside

### First Turn
- Second player channels **extra rune** during first Channel Phase

### Format
- **Best of 3** (first to 2 Set Points)
- After each game:
  - Used Battlefields are **removed**
  - Must choose from remaining Battlefields

### Battlefield Rotation
| Game | Available Battlefields |
|------|----------------------|
| 1 | All 3 |
| 2 | Remaining 2 |
| 3 | Last 1 |

## FFA3 Skirmish

**Rule refs:** 646

### Setup
- 3 players
- Each provides **3 Battlefields**
- **Randomly select 1** from each player
- 3 Battlefields total in play

### First Turn
- First player **doesn't draw** during first Draw Phase
- Last player channels **extra rune** during first Channel Phase

### Format
- **Best of 1**
- First to 8 points wins
- 2 opponents each

---

## FFA4 War

**Rule refs:** 647

### Setup
- 4 players
- Each provides **3 Battlefields**
- **First player's Battlefields are removed** (not used)
- Other 3 players randomly select 1 each
- 3 Battlefields total in play

### First Turn
- First player **doesn't draw** during first Draw Phase
- Last player channels **extra rune** during first Channel Phase

### Format
- **Best of 1**
- First to 8 points wins
- 3 opponents each

---

## 2v2 Magma Chamber

**Rule refs:** 648

### Setup
- 4 players (2 teams of 2)
- Each provides **1 Battlefield**
- **First player's Battlefield is removed**
- 3 Battlefields total in play

### Turn Order
- Alternates between teams
- Sequence: Player 1 → Opponent → Teammate → Opponent's Teammate

### First Turn
- First player **doesn't draw** during first Draw Phase
- Last player channels **extra rune** during first Channel Phase

### Format
- **Best of 1**
- First **team** to 11 points wins

## 2v2 Unique Rules

**Rule refs:** 648.8

### Teammate Interactions

| Action | Allowed? |
|--------|----------|
| Play spells on teammate's turn | Yes |
| Hide at teammate's Battlefield | No |
| Move teammate's Units | No |
| Target teammate's Units as "friendly" | Yes |

### "Friendly" Definition
- Includes your Game Objects AND teammate's Game Objects

### Relevant Players
- Teammates are **always Relevant** by default

### Communication
- Hands remain **Private Information**
- Can communicate freely (honestly or cryptically)
- Revealing cards still requires game effect

### Scoring Restrictions
- Cannot score Battlefields teammate controlled at Beginning Phase
- Prevents "retreat and conquer" strategy

### Final Point (2v2)
- Must score **all Battlefields** not held by ally at Beginning Phase
- OR score through Hold

## Victory Conditions

### Standard (All Modes)
- Reach Victory Score to win immediately

### Final Point Rules
When 1 point from Victory Score:

| Method | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Hold | Score Final Point |
| Conquer ALL Battlefields | Score Final Point |
| Conquer (not all) | Draw a card instead |

### 2v2 Final Point
- Must Conquer all Battlefields **not held by ally** at Beginning Phase
- OR Hold

## Common Questions

**Q: What's the Victory Score for 2v2?**
A: 11 points (higher because teams share score)

**Q: Can I play cards on my teammate's turn?**
A: Yes, spells can be played on teammate's turn in 2v2

**Q: Why does first player not draw in multiplayer?**
A: Balance - going first is an advantage, so they skip first draw

**Q: Can I target my teammate's units?**
A: Yes, they count as "friendly"

**Q: What happens to first player's Battlefields in FFA4/2v2?**
A: They're removed and not used that game

**Q: How do I score the final point?**
A: Hold a Battlefield, OR Conquer all Battlefields in one turn

**Q: In 2v2, can I score a Battlefield my teammate controls?**
A: No, Battlefields controlled by teammate at Beginning Phase can't be scored
