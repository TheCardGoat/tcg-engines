# Deck Building

**Topic:** Main deck, rune deck, domain identity, champion legend, signature cards, card limits
**When to Use:** Questions about building legal decks and deck construction requirements

## Overview
This index covers all rules related to constructing a legal Riftbound deck.

---

## What You Need to Play

### 1. Champion Legend
**Rule refs:** 103.1

- Placed in Legend Zone at game start
- Dictates **Domain Identity** of your Main Deck
- Cannot be removed, moved, or displaced during play

### 2. Main Deck
**Rule refs:** 103.2

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Minimum cards | 40 |
| Maximum copies of same card | 3 |
| Domain restriction | Must match Domain Identity |

**Contents:**
- 1 Chosen Champion Unit
- Units
- Gear
- Spells

### 3. Chosen Champion
**Rule refs:** 103.2.a

- Must be a **Champion Unit** (not just any unit)
- Must have a **tag matching your Champion Legend**
- Placed in Champion Zone at game start
- Counts toward the 3-copy limit

**Example:**
> Loose Cannon has the tag Jinx. You could choose Jinx, Rebel or Jinx, Demolitionist as your Chosen Champion because they also have the tag Jinx.

**Not Valid:**
> Tibbers has the tag Annie but is a Signature unit, not a Champion unit. It cannot be your Chosen Champion.

### 4. Rune Deck
**Rule refs:** 103.3

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Exact card count | 12 |
| Domain restriction | Must match Domain Identity |

- Kept separate from Main Deck
- Shuffled independently

### 5. Battlefields
**Rule refs:** 103.4

- Number determined by Mode of Play
- Subject to Domain Identity
- Typically provide 3, use 1-2 depending on mode

## Domain Identity

**Rule refs:** 103.1.b

Your Champion Legend determines which cards you can include.

### Single-Domain Cards (103.1.b.3)
- Permitted if the card's domain matches your Legend's domain

### Multi-Domain Cards (103.1.b.4)
- **ALL** domains on the card must be in your Domain Identity
- Example: A Fury/Mind card requires a Legend with both Fury AND Mind

### What Domain Identity Covers (103.1.b.2)
- Domain
- Type
- Tag
- Any other attribute specified by the Legend

---

## Signature Cards

**Rule refs:** 103.2.d

### Rules
- Maximum **3 total** Signature cards in deck
- All must have the **Champion tag matching your Champion Legend**
- Signature cards are NOT Champion units
- Cannot be placed in Champion Zone

**Example:**
> If your Champion Legend has the Annie tag, you can include up to 3 Signature cards with the Annie tag (regardless of their names).

---

## Copy Limits

**Rule refs:** 103.2.b

### Main Deck Cards
- Maximum **3 copies** of any named card
- Includes your Chosen Champion

**Example:**
> A deck could include Volibear, Furious as its Chosen Champion and still include 2 more copies of Volibear, Furious.

### Different Names = Different Cards (103.2.b.2)
Cards with different names are different cards, even if they represent the same character.

**Example:**
> A deck could include 3 copies of Yasuo, Remorseful AND 3 copies of Yasuo, Windrider because they have different names.

## Quick Checklist

- [ ] Champion Legend selected
- [ ] Chosen Champion matches Legend's tag
- [ ] Chosen Champion is a Champion Unit (not Signature)
- [ ] Main Deck has 40+ cards
- [ ] No more than 3 copies of any named card
- [ ] All cards match Domain Identity
- [ ] Rune Deck has exactly 12 runes
- [ ] Runes match Domain Identity
- [ ] No more than 3 Signature cards total
- [ ] All Signature cards match Legend's Champion tag
- [ ] Battlefields match Domain Identity
- [ ] Correct number of Battlefields for mode

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I have 4 copies of a card if one is my Chosen Champion?**
A: No, the Chosen Champion counts toward the 3-copy limit (103.2.b.1)

**Q: Can my Chosen Champion be a different character than my Legend?**
A: No, the Champion Unit must have a tag matching your Champion Legend (103.2.a.2)

**Q: Can I include a dual-domain card if I only have one of its domains?**
A: No, you need ALL domains on the card in your Domain Identity (103.1.b.4)

**Q: How many Signature cards can I have?**
A: Maximum 3 total, all matching your Legend's Champion tag (103.2.d)

**Q: Can Signature cards be my Chosen Champion?**
A: No, Signature cards are not Champion units (103.2.d.3)
