# Turn Actions

**Topic:** What you can do during your turn - playing cards, questing, challenging, inkwell, abilities
**When to Use:** Questions about what actions are available, turn sequence, and when you can do things

## Overview
This index covers all the actions you can take during your turn, including turn structure, playing cards, questing, challenging, and using abilities.

---

## Turn Structure Overview

### The Three Phases
**Summary:** Every turn has three phases in order
**Key terms:** beginning phase, main phase, end phase, turn structure
**Rule refs:** 4.1.1-4.1.4
**Details in:** Section 4: Turn Structure (4.1)

**Phases (in order):**
1. **Beginning Phase** - Ready cards, gain location lore, draw
2. **Main Phase** - Take turn actions (play cards, quest, challenge, etc.)
3. **End of Turn Phase** - Resolve end-of-turn effects, pass turn

---

## Beginning Phase

### Overview
**Summary:** Three steps to prepare for your turn
**Key terms:** ready, set, draw, beginning phase
**Rule refs:** 4.2.1-4.2.3
**Details in:** Section 4: Turn Structure (4.2)

### Step 1: Ready
**What happens:**
- Ready all your cards in play and in your inkwell
- "During your turn" effects start
- "At start of turn" effects end
- "At start of turn" abilities trigger (go to bag, resolve in Set step)

### Step 2: Set
**What happens:**
- Characters become "dry" (no longer drying) - can now quest, challenge, use ⬇ abilities
- Gain lore from locations with ◊ (happens automatically, not a triggered ability)
- Resolve all "at start of turn" abilities from the bag

### Step 3: Draw
**What happens:**
- Draw 1 card from your deck
- **Exception:** Starting player skips draw on turn 1
- Resolve any triggered effects
- Move to Main Phase

## Main Phase Turn Actions

### Overview
**Summary:** Actions you can take during your Main Phase
**Key terms:** turn actions, active player, main phase
**Rule refs:** 4.3.1-4.3.8
**Details in:** Section 4: Turn Structure (4.3)

**Available Turn Actions:**
1. Put a card into your inkwell (once per turn)
2. Play a card from your hand
3. Quest with a character
4. Challenge with a character
5. Move a character to a location
6. Use activated abilities

**Important:**
- Can take actions in any order
- Can take most actions multiple times
- Must have resources to perform actions

---

### 1. Put a Card into Inkwell
**Summary:** Add one card to your inkwell for future ink (once per turn)
**Key terms:** inkwell, ink, facedown, once per turn, inkwell symbol
**Rule refs:** 4.3.3.1-4.3.3.3
**Details in:** Section 4: Turn Structure (4.3.3)

**How to:**
1. Declare you're putting a card into inkwell
2. Choose and reveal a card from hand with inkwell symbol (⬡)
3. Place it facedown and ready in your inkwell
4. Triggered effects go to bag

**Limit:** Once per turn (can't do it twice)
**Requirement:** Card must have inkwell symbol

---

### 2. Play a Card
**Summary:** Play a card from hand by paying its cost
**Key terms:** play card, pay cost, ink cost, alternate cost, shift, singer
**Rule refs:** 4.3.4.1-4.3.4.10
**Details in:** Section 4: Turn Structure (4.3.4)

**Steps:**
1. Announce and reveal the card from hand
2. Announce how you're paying (ink cost or alternate cost)
3. Determine total cost (base + modifiers)
4. Pay the total cost
5. Card is played:
   - **Characters, Items, Locations** - Enter play
   - **Actions** - Effect resolves, then goes to discard

**Payment Options:**
- **Normal:** Pay ink cost by exerting ink cards
- **Shift:** Pay shift cost and place on top of matching character
- **Singer:** Exert a Singer character to play a song
- **Sing Together:** Exert multiple characters to sing together
- **For Free:** Some effects let you play for free (ignore ink costs)

**Important:**
- Only active player can play cards
- Usually played from hand (unless effect says otherwise)
- Characters/items/locations enter play and stay
- Actions resolve immediately and go to discard

---

### 3. Quest with a Character
**Summary:** Exert a character to gain lore
**Key terms:** quest, lore, exert, ready, dry
**Rule refs:** 4.3.5.1-4.3.5.10
**Details in:** Section 4: Turn Structure (4.3.5)

**How to Quest:**
1. Declare which character is questing
2. Check for restrictions (Reckless prevents questing)
3. Exert the character
4. Gain lore equal to character's ◊ (lore value)
5. Triggered effects go to bag

**Requirements:**
- Character must be ready
- Character must be dry (in play since beginning of turn, unless it has Rush)
- Character must not have Reckless keyword

**Goal:** Reach 20 lore to win

---

### 4. Challenge with a Character
**Summary:** Fight an opponent's character or location
**Key terms:** challenge, exert, damage, ready vs exerted, bodyguard
**Rule refs:** 4.3.6.1-4.3.6.23
**Details in:** Section 4: Turn Structure (4.3.6)

**See "Combat and Challenges" topic index for complete challenge rules**

**Quick Summary:**
1. Declare which character is challenging
2. Choose exerted opponent character (or any location)
3. Check restrictions (Bodyguard)
4. Exert your character
5. "While challenging" effects apply
6. Resolve triggered abilities
7. Challenge Damage Step - both deal damage simultaneously
8. Game state check - banish characters with damage ≥ willpower
9. Challenge ends

**Requirements:**
- Challenging character must be ready and dry
- Target character must be exerted (unless you have Evasive)
- No Bodyguard blocking (unless you have Evasive)

---

### 5. Move Character to Location
**Summary:** Pay location's move cost to move your character there
**Key terms:** move, location, move cost, your character, your location
**Rule refs:** 4.3.7.1-4.3.7.6
**Details in:** Section 4: Turn Structure (4.3.7)

**How to Move:**
1. Choose one of your characters
2. Choose one of your locations
3. Declare the move
4. Pay the location's move cost (exert ink)
5. Character moves to the location
6. Triggered effects go to bag

**Restrictions:**
- Can only move YOUR characters
- Can only move to YOUR locations
- Must pay the move cost

**Benefits:** Characters at locations may gain bonuses from location abilities

---

### 6. Use Activated Abilities
**Summary:** Pay a cost to use abilities on cards in play
**Key terms:** activated abilities, exert symbol, cost, characters, items
**Rule refs:** 4.3.8.1-4.3.8.4
**Details in:** Section 4: Turn Structure (4.3.8), Section 7: Abilities (7.5)

**Rules:**
- Characters with ⬇ abilities must be dry to use them
- Items can use activated abilities the turn they're played
- Must pay the full cost
- See Section 7.5 for detailed activated ability rules

**Common Costs:**
- ⬇ (Exert this card)
- Ink (exert ink cards)
- Discard cards
- Banish cards

---

## End of Turn Phase

### Overview
**Summary:** Resolve end-of-turn effects and pass the turn
**Key terms:** end of turn, this turn, pass turn
**Rule refs:** 4.4.1-4.4.1.4
**Details in:** Section 4: Turn Structure (4.4)

**Steps:**
1. Active player declares end of turn (no abilities waiting)
2. "At end of turn" abilities trigger and go to bag
3. Resolve all triggers in the bag
4. "This turn" effects end (e.g., Support bonuses)
5. If new triggers occurred, resolve those
6. Turn ends, next player begins

---

## Important Concepts

### "Dry" Characters
**Summary:** Characters that have been in play since beginning of your turn
**Key terms:** dry, drying, summoning sickness, in play since Set step
**Rule ref:** 4.2.2.1
**Details in:** Section 4: Turn Structure (4.2.2)

**Rules:**
- Characters "dry out" in the Set step of your Beginning Phase
- Dry characters can quest, challenge, and use ⬇ abilities
- This is like "summoning sickness" in other card games

**Exception:** Rush keyword - character can act immediately

---

### Turn Action Order
**Summary:** You choose the order of your actions
**Key terms:** any order, active player chooses
**Rule ref:** 4.3.2
**Details in:** Section 4: Turn Structure (4.3)

**Flexibility:**
- Take actions in any order you want
- Can take most actions multiple times
- Strategic: Plan your turn for maximum effect

**Example Turn:**
1. Put card into inkwell
2. Play a character
3. Quest with an old character
4. Challenge with another character
5. Use an activated ability
6. Play an action card
7. Pass turn

---

### Once Per Turn vs Unlimited
**Summary:** Some actions are limited, others are not
**Rule ref:** 4.3.2, 4.3.3
**Details in:** Section 4: Turn Structure (4.3)

**Once Per Turn:**
- Put card into inkwell (4.3.3)

**Unlimited (if you have resources):**
- Play cards
- Quest
- Challenge
- Move characters
- Use activated abilities

---

## First Turn Special Rules

### Starting Player Doesn't Draw
**Summary:** Starting player skips draw step on turn 1
**Key terms:** starting player, first turn, skip draw
**Rule ref:** 4.2.3.2
**Details in:** Section 4: Turn Structure (4.2.3)

**Why:** Balances the advantage of going first

## Common Turn Action Questions

**Q: What can I do on my first turn?**
A: All Main Phase actions, but starting player doesn't draw (4.2.3.2)

**Q: Can I quest with a character I just played?**
A: No, unless it has Rush - character must be dry (4.3.5, 10.7)

**Q: How many times can I put a card into inkwell?**
A: Once per turn (4.3.3)

**Q: Can I play multiple cards in one turn?**
A: Yes, as long as you can pay the costs (4.3.2)

**Q: When can I use my character's activated ability?**
A: If it has ⬇ in the cost, character must be dry (4.3.8.2)

**Q: Can I challenge and quest with the same character?**
A: No, both require exerting - can only do one (4.3.5, 4.3.6)

**Q: Can I quest, then challenge on the same turn with different characters?**
A: Yes, as long as each character is ready and dry

**Q: When do I gain lore from locations?**
A: During Set step of Beginning Phase (4.2.2.2)

**Q: Can I do things during my opponent's turn?**
A: No, only the active player can take turn actions (4.3.2)

## Turn Action Checklist

### Beginning Phase:
- [ ] Ready all cards
- [ ] Gain lore from locations
- [ ] Resolve "at start of turn" abilities
- [ ] Draw a card (skip if starting player on turn 1)

### Main Phase:
- [ ] Put one card into inkwell (optional, once per turn)
- [ ] Play cards by paying costs
- [ ] Quest with ready, dry characters
- [ ] Challenge with ready, dry characters
- [ ] Move characters to your locations
- [ ] Use activated abilities

### End Phase:
- [ ] Resolve "at end of turn" abilities
- [ ] "This turn" effects end
- [ ] Pass turn to next player
