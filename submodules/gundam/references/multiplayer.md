# Multiplayer Battle

Reference for Battle Royale and Team Battle modes.  
Source: Gundam Card Game Comprehensive Rules Ver. 1.5.0

---

## Modes (Section 12)

### Battle Royale

- 3+ players in a free-for-all.
- Turn order rotates clockwise.
- Each player has their own shield area, battle area, and resources.
- A defeated player is eliminated; their cards are removed from the game.
- Last player standing wins.

### Team Battle

- Players form teams (e.g., 2v2).
- Turn order alternates between teams.
- Teams may share a **shield area** (team mode variant).
- When a team's shared shields are depleted and they take battle damage, the team loses.

---

## Key Differences from Standard Play

- Multiple opponents mean attacks can target any opponent or any opponent's rested Units.
- Turn order rotates rather than alternating.
- Some card effects may reference "each opponent" or "all opponents" — resolve for each applicable player.
- Team Battle introduces coordination between teammates but does not allow sharing of hand, resources, or battle area.
