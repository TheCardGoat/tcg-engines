# 1. Game Overview

## 1-1. Number of Players

### 1-1-1.

Gundam Card Game is a competitive game between two players. Refer to multiplayer rules for other player counts.

## 1-2. Winning and Losing the Game

### 1-2-1.

When either player is defeated, the game ends. The remaining player wins.

### 1-2-2. Defeat Conditions

- **1-2-2-1.** Taking battle damage with no shields → defeat
- **1-2-2-2.** No cards in deck → defeat

### 1-2-3.

Defeat is processed during rules management.

### 1-2-4.

A player may concede at any time → immediate loss.

### 1-2-5.

No card effect can force concession.

## 1-3. Fundamental Game Rules

- Card text overrides rules
- Perform actions as much as possible
- Impossible actions are skipped
- Active effects override required actions
- Active player resolves first in simultaneous choices
- Numbers must be whole ≥1 unless stated otherwise
- Negative values → treated as 0
- Effects resolve in written order

# 3. Card Types

## 3-1. Overview

Five card types total.

## 3-2. Units

- Deployed to battle area
- Only cards that can attack
- Have AP & HP
- Cannot attack first turn (unless linked)

## 3-3. Pilots

- Paired under Units
- Add stats/effects
- Move with Unit

## 3-4. Commands

- One-time effects
- Sent to trash after resolution
- May have 【Pilot】 or 【Burst】

## 3-5. Bases

- Placed in shield area
- Take damage before shields

## 3-6. Resources

- Used to pay costs

# 5. Essential Game Terminology

## Key Terms

- **Effect**: Card abilities
- **Player**: Card owner
- **Active/Rested**: Card orientation
- **Damage**: Tracked via counters
- **Destroy**: HP reaches 0
- **Discard**: Hand → trash
- **Remove**: To removal area
- **Draw**: Deck → hand
- **Shuffle**: Randomize deck

## Tokens

- Special cards created by effects
- Removed when leaving valid zones

# 13. Keyword Effects and Keywords

## Keyword Effects

- **<Repair>**: Heal at end of turn
- **<Breach>**: Damage shields after kill
- **<Support>**: Buff allies
- **<Blocker>**: Redirect attacks
- **<First Strike>**: Deal damage first
- **<High-Maneuver>**: Cannot be blocked
- **<Suppression>**: Hit multiple shields
